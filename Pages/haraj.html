Haraj Al Fajr

Haraj Al Fajr is an intuitive application designed to connect users with local markets, allowing them to search and purchase a variety of products. The app also offers the option to pre-order directly from suppliers, ensuring that fresh products are delivered within 24 hours. Haraj Al Fajr streamlines the shopping experience, providing convenience and freshness with every order.
