A-Z Trajet

At AZ Trajet, a VTC platform developed to address the growing demand for transportation in Algeria, we created a seamless solution that connects passengers with drivers through an intuitive interface. AZ Trajet offers real-time ride-hailing and carpooling options, providing a flexible experience for users whether they are commuting or planning trips. With features like fare negotiation, ride tracking, and a smooth payment system, the platform ensures efficiency for both riders and drivers. Its user-friendly design includes easy ride acceptance, driver status management, and the ability to terminate rides upon completion, offering a streamlined experience tailored to the Algerian market.
