Al Razi

At Al Razi, we developed a comprehensive Patient Record Management (PRM) SaaS platform to streamline the healthcare experience for both patients and doctors. The platform consists of a user-friendly mobile app for patients and an intuitive dashboard for healthcare providers. Through the patient app, users can easily schedule appointments, view medical documents such as prescriptions and test results, and manage their treatment plans. On the doctor’s dashboard, healthcare professionals can manage appointments, view patient histories, and track daily schedules. The interface is designed to simplify administrative tasks, allowing doctors to focus more on patient care. It provides a calendar for easy management of the day’s bookings.
