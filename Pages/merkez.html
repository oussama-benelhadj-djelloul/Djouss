MERKEZ

At MERKEZ, a platform developed in collaboration with a client from France, we created an immersive solution to support immigrants in learning Arabic and the Quran. MERKEZ offers a seamless and accessible learning experience for all levels, from beginners to advanced learners. With its user-friendly design, the platform provides a smooth onboarding process, personalized dashboards, and interactive modules, empowering users to master the Arabic language and deepen their Quranic knowledge.
